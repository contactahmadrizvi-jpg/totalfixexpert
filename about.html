About Fix Experts Oman

Pioneering premium home services, professional shifting, and custom woodwork standards across the Sultanate of Oman.

Our Journey

Crafting Trust Since 2016

Fix Experts Oman (Total Fix Expert Oman) began with a single truck and a simple vision: to eliminate the unreliability of home maintenance services in Muscat. By recruiting certified carpenters, trained logistics experts, and experienced contractors, we built a brand defined by high-precision outcomes.

Today, we operate a fleet of closed dust-proof shifting trucks and a state-of-the-art carpentry workshop. We service residential villas, corporate offices, and local shops with dedication and focus on client satisfaction.

Our Mission

To provide homeowners and corporate entities across Oman with stress-free relocations, high-end renovations, and reliable custom woodwork solutions executed by certified professionals.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the absolute gold standard for premium moving and handyman solutions in Oman, setting industry benchmarks for safety, speed, and design craftsmanship.
